What It Is


Maine State of Learning is a project fueled by public and private partnerships across the state of Maine to provide more learning opportunities to Maine citizens of all ages, recognize that learning through digital badges, and connect it to statewide proficiencies, career pathways and personal goals.

Personal, professional and community growth.

Who Benefits


MAINERS

Maine citizens of all ages will have access to more opportunities for growth and success.

LEARNING PROVIDERS

Providers across the state will reach a broader audience, connect to other providers and better understand their learners.

EMPLOYERS

Local businesses and employers can on-ramp citizens into jobs and careers.
